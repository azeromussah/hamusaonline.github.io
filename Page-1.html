FAQ

What is your design process like?

Clients often want to understand the steps involved in the design process. This question allows the graphic designer to explain how they gather information, conceptualize ideas, create drafts, and refine designs based on client feedback. It provides insight into their workflow and ensures alignment with the client's expectations.

What is your approach to understanding a client's brand or project requirements?

Clarity on a designer's approach to understanding a client's brand or project is crucial. This question helps clients gauge the designer's ability to comprehend their vision, target audience, and overall goals. It allows the designer to discuss methods such as client interviews, research, and mood boards that contribute to a thorough understanding of the project.

How do you handle revisions or changes to the initial design?

Clients often want to know the flexibility and responsiveness of a graphic designer when it comes to revisions. This question allows the designer to explain their policy on revisions, including the number of revisions included in the project, the communication process during revisions, and how additional changes beyond the agreed-upon scope are handled. It helps manage expectations and ensures a smooth collaboration process.
